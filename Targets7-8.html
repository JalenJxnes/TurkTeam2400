Economic Rights

Economic culture in Turkey is a male-lead environment. Women have little to absolutely no power or rights when it comes to economic standards such as land ownership or the trading of economic resources. The gender cuture in Turkey also represents an extreme gap between male and female pay in all areas of business and the work place. This campaign is to ensure that women are able to be seen as equal financial owners and prospects to men.

Technological Advancements

Gender equality fighters are turning to technology for the source of the solution to the inequality in Turkey. Women are finding new ways through technology to make their voices heard and their reputations seen. Many women are finding their purpose in computer programming and finding jobs through Microsoft where they are receiving equal pay and equal opportunity opposed to what is available in their nation.
