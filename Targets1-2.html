Education in Turkey

In the country of Turkey education is granted to whoever wishes to take advantage of it, whether male or female. But females are less likely to attend or even finish school. It is estimated that sixteen million girls will never get to attend school, this is for many reasons but the main one being poverty. Although Turkey is a first-world country many of its people are poor.

Another reason is that the parents who are of the older generation think that a woman is only fit to be a housewife and they see that getting an education is useless. When these young girls are surrounded by discouraging individuals, they no longer continue to pursue an education and will eventually get pressured into getting married and building a family.
